# Statistical Methods

## Descriptive Statistics
Used to understand respondent demographics and channel preferences.

## Correlation Analysis
Examined relationships between sustainability attitudes and fast-fashion perceptions.

## Independent Sample T-Test
Compared Generation Z and older respondents.

## Repeated Measures ANOVA
Investigated differences across product categories and shopping drivers.

## Linear Regression
Examined predictors of sustainability perceptions.

## Binary Logistic Regression
Predicted offline versus online channel preference.

## Multinomial Logistic Regression
Modelled offline, online and hybrid shopping behaviour.
